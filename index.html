Company Information

Cousteau Services Limited (CSL) has been in operation since 2013. CSL is a sales and service oriented organization specializing in eSecurity (Electronic Securities) and Technology Services. We are also known as a Value Added Reseller for the following security brands:

  1. Bioidentidad, the Regional Distributor for Suprema Biometric Technologies
  2. Eclipse CCTV Surveillance Equipment

Cousteau Services Limited was formed with the intention of supplying eSecurity and technological services to a wide cross section of business and residential clients. We forsaw the need to focus our efforts and energy on achieving excellence in customer service and at the same time providing value for money.

Cousteau's technicians, inclusive of being CANASA certified, are certified as programmers and installers for the following brands:

  • PELCO
  • Quaddrix
  • DSC
  • Suprema
  • Notifier

Located in Central, Cousteau is available to provide you with service at any point of our isle.
