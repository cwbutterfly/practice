Intrusion Alarm

Intrusion Alarms are available in wired and wireless systems. Wired systems have proven to be more reliable, but with the advances in wireless tecnology in recent times, wireless systems are proving to be just as good and easier to install and configure than wired systems.

Access Control Systems

As the name implies access control systems are systems that allow an Administrator to control access to secure areas. Access Control can be as simple as a lock and key, or the more advanced systems that allow a user to gain access via a code, prox card, biometrics or a combination of the three.

CCTV System

These systems come in a variety of protocols and configurations. Available HD protocols are AHD, CVI, TVI, SDI(DVR's) and IP(NVR). DVR's come in 4 Channel, 8 Channel and 16 Channel systems and the NVR"s are available in 8, 16, 32, 64 and up depending on the CMS software.>
